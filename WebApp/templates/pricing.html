Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, all paid plans can be cancelled anytime. Free plan has no commitment.

What AI models are used?

We use OpenRouter to access 200+ models including Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, and more. Pro users get priority access to premium models.

Is this legal advice?

No. This is a document preparation tool only. Always review documents with a qualified attorney before filing. No attorney-client relationship is created.

Which states are supported?

Currently: Alabama, California, Texas, New York, Florida, and Illinois. Each state has 10 document types (60+ total templates).

How does citation verification work?

Every case citation is checked against CourtListener's 9M+ case database. Hallucinated citations (that don't exist) are flagged before you file.
